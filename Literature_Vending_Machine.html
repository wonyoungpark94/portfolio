Literature Vending Machine

2018 Spring

Literature vending machine is the product that prints on receipts of literary works such as poetry, novels, and essays. It is designed to allow the public to enjoy literary works without carrying books. If you press a button on the machine, part of the literature works of Korean and foreign authors are printed randomly. Short text (500 characters or less), long text (2000 characters maximum) can be selected. Patent and trade mark application have been completed.

Video

Data

The literary vending machine contains more than 2000 literary works. It is the result of partnering with literature publishing house, MunhakDongnae, and Minumsa. Users can enjoy literary works that are printed randomly in literary vending machines with various literary works. Through this partnership with more publishers, works of literature will be more diverse in the future.

Process

Modeling

I designed literature vending machine in square shape to give a Korean impression. After a quick sketch, I modeled it as SolidWorks to work with it in 3D. As the main designer, I was did all of sketch, concept design and 3d modeling.

Keyshot

Trial Version

This is rendering of trial version. The main body has a long rectangular shape. In order to achieve the overall harmony, the signboard was also designed in square form. I tried various ways such as screwing the signboard from the back and putting it on the top. But the size of the main body is too small and the signboard looks unstable. So, I once again improved the design.

Final Design

This is the rendering of the final design. The area of the main body is slightly wider and the signboard is screwed directly onto the main body. It is for durability. When users choose one of the long and short texts, LED will flash for giving feedback to them. Once the button is pressed, the literature work is printed on the front of the enclosure.

Assemble

This is a photograph of the assembly process. After the exterior parts are manufactured, the engineering parts inside are assembled. The LattePanda is used to print literature works on the receipt.

Previous Design

I also participated in this version as a main designer too. I took sketch, design and modeling. Iron plate is bent and two parts of color mdf are attached to side parts.

My Role

I participated in this project as a main designer. Our team consisted of one engineer, one engineer and one designer. I was the only designer. I did all of concept sketching, design, rendering and modeling.
