Interactive News Printer

2017 fall [IDP2 class, UNIST CDE]

A lot of news comes out every day. Economy, Life, Sports and even Entertainment News are released and disappeared every minutes and every hours. The Purpose of work is changing easily consumed news contents into design item which want to store and collect.

‘In a flood of content, what if we change news contents into a design item that people want to keep?’
It is an 'interactive news printer' that makes graphic effects with user's gesture to the latest news letters, then prints it in tangible post card form. We add interactive elements not only for making news contents easier to access but also transforming them into design items that people want to possess.

Hand motion sensor (leap motion) located on the top of the product detects height and locate of user's hand, so user can change graphics and color of news contents’ background. The user can check the changing graphic effect in real time, through the screen projected by beam projector in the front. When user presses the button on the top of the product, customized news with hand gesture is printed.

Concept Generation

Card Sorting

For generating concept, cart sorting method is used, which is a relatively low-tech approach. Each of us gathers over 15 images related with jtbc’s brand identity, writes down simple description and prints them all. Then, categorizes similar things and extract keywords. The result is shown below.

    Key words
  • Human Motion Capture
  • Color Composition
  • Patterned Artifact
  • Specific Function
  • Calm Steady Pool

3D Rendering

Using solid works, fast rendering is progressed for communicating our concept design with others who take same course and can give us lots of feedback. The first form is designed as a square case, and the screen part is also designed with square angles. After feedbacks process, the table form is chosen rather than the barrel case. As a whole, the table and screen both have rounds in the corners, resulting in a soft feel.

Scaled Prototyping (1:10)

It is proceed prior to the making process. Scaled prototyping is a very important process because there is a big difference between what we see on the screen and what we actually see in the real world. The laser cutting is used to cut and assemble the color mdf, and the portion to made with pipe is made with 3d printer. As a result of the scaled prototype, We notice that the pipe diameter is very thin and change pipe to thicker one.
